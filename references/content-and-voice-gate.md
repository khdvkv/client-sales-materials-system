# Content and Voice Gate

## Purpose

Turn source evidence into final client-facing copy for the actual sales material. The output must help the intended reader decide or act; it cannot describe the team's production process.

## Inputs

- accepted material job, audience, channel and next action;
- complete best-in-class sequence receipt for the same document job;
- source and claim register;
- client vocabulary and legal/privacy constraints;
- accepted storyline or information hierarchy.

## Page-level copy card

For every page or section record:

```markdown
Reader question:
Action title or conclusion:
Evidence:
Visible body copy:
Caption or qualification:
Visual information object:
Next action:
Sources and freshness:
```

`Visual information object` is mandatory before layout. It names a comparison, annotated photograph, route, map, timeline, decision tree, table, calculation, evidence crop, form, diagram or another inspectable object. “Cards with text” is not an information object.

## Voice sequence

1. Preserve source facts, qualifications and client terms.
2. Apply `paper-planes-copy-chief` as the primary Russian client-copy owner.
3. Apply `balakhnin-voice` only as a register source: direct thesis, mechanism, causal chain, density, stable object names and proof discipline.
4. Apply Sergey hard lints. They override corpus markers, including negative parallelisms that may appear in Balakhnin's Telegram corpus.
5. Run `text-deai-editor` for ritual openings, abstract consultant nouns, repeated three-part formulas, decorative English, empty confidence and synthetic transitions.
6. Read the whole material as one sequence and remove term drift, duplicated conclusions and mismatched CTA.

## Hard failures

- visible copy explains what the future material should contain;
- internal stages, codes, source policies, QA notes or production recommendations appear in the client file;
- claims lack an approved source or visible qualification;
- one client object receives several decorative synonyms;
- headings are topics rather than conclusions or reader promises;
- prose replaces a useful information object;
- the copy imitates an individual author or claims their authorship;
- Paper Planes or personal contacts appear without explicit client-facing purpose and approval.
